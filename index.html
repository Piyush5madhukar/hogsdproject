First YEAR

When you don’t make it to a college of renown, the pressure to outperform others as well as yourself starts mounting up. I initially used to cringe and complain but soon I started participating in competitions, attending events, seminars, fests and what not!

First semester Memories

Firstly, I was a complete introvert, I am not used to talk to people much, I didn’t have interesting topics to talk about.It is the first thing you have to do than being obident, kind, happy. Know your capacity to be self dependent, with positive attitude.

Second Semester Memories

If you have the will to study, you’ll automatically be motivated.You are on your own now, in ways you probably haven’t been before. It will be tempting to fit in with the crowd. Try to be yourself, make lifelong friends and remember why you are there. College is about learning, but also about growing socially. Enjoy yourself!
